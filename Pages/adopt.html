Adopt a Dog!

Give your future best friend a loving home!

What to do when you adopt a dog

  • Visit the shelter or adoption center to meet the dog
  • Ask for the dog's medical and behavioral history
  • Make sure your home is dog friendly. This can include making sure any toxic plants are removed. Advice can be taken from the adoption center employees.
  • Prepare the basic supplies, like food, water, leash, collar, and anything else recommended by the adoption center.
  • Schedule a visit with the vet for a general health check.

How to take care of your dog

  • Feed a balanced diet appropriate for their age and breed.
  • Ensure daily exercise and playtime to keep them physically and mentally healthy.
  • Provide regular grooming and check for fleas/ticks.
  • Train and socialize your dog to improve behavior and comfort.
  • Take your dog for regular vet checkups and vaccinations.

REMEMBER

Before adopting, it is your responsibility to ask all necessary questions regarding the dog's background, health, behavior, and the adoption process. Make sure you fully understand the commitment you're making. Ask about return policies, medical needs, and any adoption fees or requirements.
